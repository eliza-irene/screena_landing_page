What is screena?

screena builds an anonymous persona for candidates via a research based skill evaluation. When an employer searches for candidates or posts a job, they're presented with qualified, vetted, and easy to read personas. Once the employer decides to move forward, the identity of the potential new potential employee is revealed. With the candidate’s permission, of course. It's like a dating game, but our algorithm makes far better matches than Chuck Woolery.

Level the playing field.

Find candidates with the right skills and results, not the ones with the right keywords. Don't use a robot or data center to do your recruiting, you might miss the best candidates. Not every person you want to hire know the intricacies of a complicated screening algorithm, and that should not be the reason to miss out on good people.
